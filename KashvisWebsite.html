About Us

Who We Are

What Haven Health Initiative Is

Haven Health Initiative is a student-led nonprofit that helps uninsured and underinsured patients find affordable healthcare, understand their options, and follow through on getting care.

Here's what we do in plain terms: people who can't afford healthcare reach out to us. We tell them the cheapest place nearby to get the care they need. We make sure they actually go. We track whether it worked.

We combine financial cost analysis, medical guidance, and a technology-powered accountability system to make sure patients don't just know where to go — they actually get there.

Who We Serve

We serve uninsured and underinsured patients in the Edison, NJ area and surrounding communities. Anyone who cannot afford standard healthcare costs is welcome to reach out — no documentation required.

How It Works

Three Simple Steps

1

Fill out our form

Tell us a little about your situation — what care you need, your location, and your budget. It takes about 3 minutes and is completely free. No insurance required.

Go to the form →
2

We call you within 24 hours

One of our team members will call you within 24 hours of your form submission. We'll ask a few questions to understand your situation and figure out exactly what type of care you need.

3

We match you to affordable care and make sure you get there

Within 2 hours of your intake call, we send you the clinic name, address, cash price, and phone number — the most affordable option for your situation. We'll even help you book if you need it.

Then we send you reminders before your appointment and follow up 7 days after to make sure you got the care you needed.

Important: We never diagnose. We are not doctors. We are a healthcare navigation service — we help you find the right professional and make sure you get there.
